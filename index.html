About Me

I'm proficient in Full Stack Development, Python, and AI, with a keen interest in leveraging technology for real-world impact.
Leading the launch of WESAT(Women Engineered Satellite), Kerala's First Student Satellite and India's First Women-Only Mission, was a significant milestone. Managing the project from development to launch with a talented team of students was both challenging and rewarding. This experience reinforced the importance of diversity in STEM and showcased my leadership and project management skills. In my projects, I prioritize solving practical problems. For instance, I developed a real-time data visualization website for WESAT payload monitoring, ensuring seamless integration and user experience. I also created a Chrome extension to optimize file management, addressing a common user pain point.
My involvement as Campus Lead for Tinkerhub LBSITW led to a 200% increase in community participation. Organizing events like 'Let's Tinker' fostered collaboration and innovation among tech enthusiasts. As a Student Representative for IEEE PIE Kerala Section, I lead a diverse team curating events to promote innovation and entrepreneurship.
My contributions have been recognized with awards such as the Outstanding Student Volunteer Award by IEEE PIE Kerala Section and the Sakhi StreeSakthi Award by The Engineers Federation, Kerala Water Authority. I've also spoken at prestigious events like the IEDC Summit, sharing insights and inspiring others in the tech community. Beyond technical skills, I'm committed to empowering students, especially girls, in tech through mentoring and initiatives like 'Project Ganitham.' I believe in creating a positive impact through leadership and innovation.
